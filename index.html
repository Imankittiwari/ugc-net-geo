TRICK 10 | CORAL REEF SEQUENCE
Darwin's Subsidence Theory
"First Born, Adult Later" = Fringing → Barrier → Atoll
Fringing: Attached to coast, No lagoon (Sri Lanka, Red Sea)
Barrier: Island subsides, Lagoon forms (Great Barrier Reef, Australia)
Atoll: Island fully sinks, Ring of reef + lagoon (Maldives)

Remember: Atoll = no island, only ring!

TRICK 12 | PLATE BOUNDARIES
3 Types — C-D-T
"Continents Diverge and Transform"
Convergent (C): Plates meet → Fold Mountains (Himalayas) / Trenches / Volcanoes
Divergent (D): Plates apart → Mid-ocean ridges, New crust (Mid-Atlantic Ridge)
Transform (T): Plates slide sideways → Earthquakes only (San Andreas Fault, California)
TRICK 13 | PRESSURE BELTS
Position + Wind
0°=Hot ITCZ → 30°=Horse High → 60°=Sub-polar Low → 90°=Polar High
ITCZ (0°): Hot rises → Low → Doldrums + Rain
Sub-tropical High (30°): Air descends → Hot Deserts (Sahara, Thar)
Sub-polar Low (60°): Air rises → Cyclones + Storms
Polar High (90°): Cold air → Polar Easterlies

Winds between belts: Trade (0-30°), Westerlies (30-60°), Polar Easterlies (60-90°)
TRICK 14 | SAMPLING METHODS
Types याद करो
"SMART" = Systematic, Multi-stage, Accidental, Random, Targeted(Stratified)
Simple Random: Lottery। Most unbiased।
Systematic: Every nth element (every 10th)।
Stratified: Strata बनाओ → random from each। Best for heterogeneous।
